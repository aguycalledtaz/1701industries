About 1701

Simple to explain. Serious in ambition.

1701 Industries is a Lisbon-based company focused on design-led spaces, sustainable infrastructure, and commercially strong concepts that connect Europe, the UK, and Japan.

What the company is

A design-led platform for modern projects.

1701 Industries brings together infrastructure, technology, customer experience, and cultural value in one clear business direction. It is built for projects that need more than a standard property, technology, or brand-led approach on its own.

The company is designed to develop high-quality environments and concepts that are commercially viable, visually refined, and relevant to how people live, work, and engage today.

Why it matters

Because space, energy, and experience now overlap.

Old models often separate infrastructure from design and customer experience. 1701 works on the assumption that these should be connected. A space should function better, feel better, and deliver stronger value at the same time.

This is relevant for investors, collaborators, public bodies, and commercial partners looking for projects with clearer positioning and stronger long-term logic.

Core areas

What sits inside the 1701 model.

Infrastructure

Design-led thinking around solar, battery storage, software, smart systems, and future-facing building logic.

Commercial space

High-quality environments that combine brand experience, usability, and commercial strength.

Culture

Projects and ventures that create stronger emotional connection through design, hospitality, wellness, and storytelling.

Why Lisbon

A strategic European base.

Lisbon gives 1701 Industries a practical position for European operations while supporting wider links into the UK and Japan. It is a strong base for partnerships, venture building, grant opportunities, and future growth.

Cross-border position

Europe, UK, and Japan.

1701 is designed to bridge ideas, markets, and partnerships across regions. That international orientation is part of the company’s value, not just a background detail.
