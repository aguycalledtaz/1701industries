About

1701 Industries is a design-led company with a clear cross-border purpose.

The business brings together premium design thinking, sustainable infrastructure, commercial strategy, and culture so projects feel coherent, modern, and commercially credible.

What the company is

1701 Industries develops concepts, ventures, and environments where design quality and practical infrastructure work together.

What the company works around

Projects may involve solar, batteries, software, smart systems, hospitality, premium experience, and culture-led commercial strategy.

What the company aims to do

Create spaces and systems that generate both commercial value and stronger day-to-day usefulness for people and places.

Why the model matters

One direction instead of disconnected parts.

Too many projects separate architecture, infrastructure, brand, customer experience, and commercial strategy. 1701 approaches those things together. That is where better outcomes often come from: stronger identity, better usability, better long-term value, and clearer rollout thinking.

The result is a model suited to premium environments, modern infrastructure thinking, and internationally connected ventures.

Why Lisbon

European base. International reach.

Lisbon provides a practical base inside Europe while supporting international collaboration across the UK and Japan. It gives 1701 a credible platform for partnerships, growth, and future funding pathways.

Who this helps

Clear for investors, partners, and the public.

The company is designed to make sense to funders, public bodies, property owners, collaborators, and customers. The message remains simple while the capability remains broad.
