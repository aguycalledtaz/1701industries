Founding

Why 1701.

1701 Industries was formed in Lisbon to create a clearer and more credible operating platform for work spanning Portugal, London access, and Japan.

The founding rationale is simple: establish one disciplined company platform that can support serious conversations with clients, partners, investors, and collaborators across those markets.

The company emerged from earlier venture work and from the recognition that discussions across Portugal, London access, and Japan required a more coherent structure. Rather than operating through fragmented concepts, 1701 provides a single corporate platform with a clearer commercial identity.

The founding idea was straightforward: combine design-led thinking with practical execution. That means linking sustainable infrastructure, technology, systems, and modern user-facing concepts in a way that is commercially understandable and strategically useful.

Lisbon was chosen not only as an address, but as an operating base. It offers a European foundation for company building, cross-border coordination, and long-term venture development while maintaining access to the UK and relevance to Japanese partnerships.

Today, 1701 Industries exists to support serious commercial conversations and to create ventures, spaces, and platforms that are coherent in both form and function.

In practical terms, 1701 was created as the business platform behind the wider story: a company base that makes the founding vision easier to understand, present, and grow.

Key founding points

Built in Lisbon as a strategic EU base
Created to support Portugal, UK, and Japan discussions
Developed from earlier cross-sector venture work
Focused on clear business positioning and execution
