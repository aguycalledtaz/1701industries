Legacy

From Satēra (iO) to 1701 Industries

1701 Industries grew out of Satēra (iO), a London-based platform developed around sustainability, energy systems, and a more integrated infrastructure model. 1701 carries that earlier ambition forward in a broader, more structured corporate form rooted in Lisbon.

Founders

Built from cross-sector experience

The original vision was shaped by Taz Kha'lique, with an Apple design and technology background, and Sita Sojitra, with a BAE Systems engineering and project management background, alongside wider expertise and manufacturing partners.
